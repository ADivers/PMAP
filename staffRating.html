Conversion of Elements to Summary Ratings

Description

After rating and assigning a score to each critical element, the total points are divided by the number of Critical Elements to arrive at an average score (up to two decimal places). This score will be converted to a summary rating based on the following point values:

  • Level 5: Achieved outstanding Results (AO)
  • Level 4: Achieved More than Expected Results (AM)
  • Level 3: Achieved Expected Results (AE)
  • Level 2: Partially Achieved Expected Results (PA)
  • Level 1: Achieved Unsatisfactory Results (UR)
